About us

We are a local, family run business which provides a professional, cost effective accounting solution. As qualified accountants, our aim is to take care of your financial and accounting operations affording you the time and peace of mind to run your business. We provide a friendly and flexible service to sole traders, partnerships and small limited companies.

Ben Latimer

Ben qualified as a chartered accountant in 2006 after graduating from Portsmouth University with a 1st class degree in Economics. His focus is on owner managed business support including accountancy, management accounts, business planning and personal taxation.

Matthew Latimer

Matthew has worked in the specialist area of Corporation tax since 2004. He was previously employed by Baker Tilly and has developed a breadth of expertise across audit, accountancy and business taxation.
